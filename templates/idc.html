IDC Overview

The IDC Programme is designed to equip learners with essential digital, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills needed to prepare for future opportunities in technology and innovation.

Programme Objectives

  • Develop digital and technical skills.
  • Improve problem-solving and analytical thinking.
  • Prepare learners for technology-focused assessments and careers.
  • Build confidence through practical learning activities.

What the Programme Includes

Digital Skills Training

Learners are introduced to important digital tools and technologies that help improve productivity, communication, and technical understanding.

Critical Thinking Development

The programme includes activities and lessons that strengthen critical thinking and decision-making abilities through real-world problem-solving exercises.

Practice Activities

Learners complete guided practice exercises and simulations that help prepare them for assessments and future technology learning opportunities.

  • Practice real-world scenarios.
  • Strengthen logical reasoning skills.
  • Improve confidence and readiness.

Benefits of the Programme

  • Gain valuable digital and problem-solving skills.
  • Improve confidence in technology-related tasks.
  • Develop critical thinking and communication abilities.
  • Prepare for future education and career opportunities.
