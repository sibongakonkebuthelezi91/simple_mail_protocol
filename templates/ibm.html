IMB Skill Courses Overview

The IMB Skill Courses are designed to help learners strengthen the key skills needed to succeed in the WeThinkCode (WTC) online assessment process. These short courses focus on improving problem-solving, critical thinking, and test preparation abilities through guided learning and practical exercises.

Course Objectives

  • Develop and enhance problem-solving skills.
  • Improve critical thinking and analytical abilities.
  • Prepare learners for the WTC online assessment test format.
  • Build confidence through guided practice and simulations.

What the Course Includes

Problem Solving Course

This short course is designed to help you begin developing and improving your problem-solving abilities. Problem-solving is one of the most important skills required during the WTC online assessment and in software development careers.

Critical Thinking Video Lessons

The course includes a series of videos aimed at strengthening your critical thinking capacity. These lessons help learners approach challenges logically, analyze information effectively, and make informed decisions during assessments.

WTC Practice Test

A practice test created by the WTC Student Recruitment Team is included to simulate the real WTC online assessment experience.

  • Familiarize yourself with the test structure.
  • Practice under realistic conditions.
  • Identify areas that need improvement before attempting the actual assessment.

Benefits of the Course

  • Gain confidence in solving complex problems.
  • Improve logical reasoning and decision-making skills.
  • Better understand what to expect during the WTC assessment.
  • Increase readiness for future technology-related learning opportunities.
