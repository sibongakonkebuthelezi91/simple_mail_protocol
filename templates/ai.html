GenAI Courses Overview

The GenAI Course is designed to introduce learners to Generative Artificial Intelligence and how it can be used to solve real-world problems, improve productivity, and build modern digital skills for the future.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the basics of Generative AI.
  • Learn how AI tools can assist in problem-solving.
  • Develop practical skills using AI-powered platforms.
  • Prepare for future AI-driven careers and opportunities.

What the Course Includes

Introduction to AI

This section explains what Artificial Intelligence is, how it works, and how it is used in everyday applications such as chatbots, recommendation systems, and automation tools.

Hands-on AI Tools

Learners get practical experience using GenAI tools to create content, solve problems, and improve productivity in real-world scenarios.

AI Practice Activities

The course includes exercises that help learners apply AI concepts in simple tasks, improving understanding and confidence.

  • Create AI-generated content.
  • Solve guided problem-solving tasks using AI.
  • Explore real-life AI use cases.

Benefits of the Course

  • Gain future-ready AI skills.
  • Improve problem-solving and creativity.
  • Understand how AI is used in modern industries.
  • Build confidence in using digital tools.
